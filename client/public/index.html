About Us

WHAT IS VONG?

VONG (Voice Of the New Generation) is a dynamic initiative under Omysha foundation that empowers and amplifies the voices of the youth. Through education, engagement, and advocacy, VONG aims to create a sustainable future by equipping the new generation with the necessary knowledge and skills to drive positive change.

Our Services

What VONG Provides

Educational Workshops
Community Engagement
Advocacy and Policy Engagement
Youth Leadership Development
Global Youth Summits

  • VONG conducts educational workshops aimed at empowering the youth with knowledge and skills related to sustainable development and the SDGs. These workshops provide a platform for interactive learning, discussions, and hands-on activities, enabling participants to gain a deeper understanding of global challenges and develop the necessary tools to address them.

    Informative sessions Interactive learning Hands-on activities

  • VONG actively engages with local communities to drive sustainable change at the grassroots level. Through community-based initiatives, VONG promotes environmental awareness, encourages sustainable practices, and fosters a sense of responsibility towards the planet.

    Clean-up drives Recycling initiatives Awareness campaign

  • VONG advocates for the implementation of sustainable policies and actively engages with policymakers to drive positive change. By raising awareness, providing input, and collaborating with other organizations, VONG seeks to influence policy decisions and promote the adoption of sustainable practices.

    Policy advocacy Stakeholder engagement Awareness raising

  • Youth Leadership Development: VONG focuses on developing leadership skills and empowering young individuals to become effective change agents. Through leadership programs and mentorship initiatives, VONG nurtures the potential of the new generation to drive sustainable initiatives in their communities.

    Leadership programs Empowerment Collaborative partnerships

  • VONG organizes global youth summits to bring together young change-makers from diverse backgrounds. These summits provide a platform for networking, knowledge exchange, and collaboration on innovative solutions to global sustainability challenges.

    Networking opportunities Knowledge exchange Collaborative projects
